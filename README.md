# LITHOMANCY

Whispered in stone - a tiny browser-born experiment in rune-play.

Summon, shape, and unleash the memory of rocks. Combine stones and runes to coax spells from the ground; every touch rearranges the lattice of possibility.

## What it is

- Lithomancy is a browser-based online 1v1 dueling game. Cast spells, concoct potions, and harness charms, to read your opponent and outmaneuver them in a wizardly reimagining of rock paper scissors.

Built with TypeScript, Tailwind, and WebSocket
