◈ How to Play ◈

Goal: Control 4 consecutive turrets on the ring of 8 to win.

Each turn: Both players are dealt 5 stones. Each stone shows either a Shape (▲ ◻ ⬟ ⬡ ⯃) or a Number (3 4 5 6 8). You may then recast (reroll) any stones once.

Hand Types (shapes vs numbers):

  • ✨ Spell — 3 shapes + 2 numbers. Power = sum of the 2 numbers.
  • ⚗️ Potion — 4 shapes + 1 number. Potency = the number.
  • ⚗️ Full Potion — 5 shapes. Beats any regular Potion.
  • 🔮 Charm — 4 numbers + 1 shape. Rating = sides on the shape.
  • 🔮 Full Charm — 5 numbers. Beats any regular Charm.
  • 💨 Bungle — 2 shapes + 3 numbers. Loses to everything.

Cycle: ✨ Spell beats ⚗️ Potion · ⚗️ Potion beats 🔮 Charm · 🔮 Charm beats ✨ Spell

Full beats Regular of the same type. Higher value wins ties of the same type.

Winner's Action:

  • ✨ Spell — Place one of your gems on any empty turret.
  • ⚗️ Potion — Swap your gem with an adjacent opponent gem.
  • 🔮 Charm — Remove one of your opponent's gems from the board.
