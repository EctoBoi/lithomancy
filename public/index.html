◈ How to Play ◈

Goal: Control 4 consecutive turrets on the tower of 8 to win.

Each turn: Both players are dealt 5 stones. Each stone shows either a Shape (▲ ◻ ⬟ ⬡ ⯃) or a Star (4 5 8 9 10 points). You may then recast (reroll) any stones once.

Hand Types (shapes vs stars):

  • ✨ Spell — 3 shapes + 2 stars. Power = sum of the 2 star points.
  • ⚗️ Potion — 4 shapes + 1 star. Potency = the star points.
  • ⚗️ Full Potion — 5 shapes. Beats any regular Potion.
  • 🔮 Charm — 4 stars + 1 shape. Rating = sides on the shape.
  • 🔮 Full Charm — 5 stars. Beats any regular Charm.
  • 💨 Bungle — 2 shapes + 3 stars. Loses to everything.

Cycle: ✨ Spell beats ⚗️ Potion · ⚗️ Potion beats 🔮 Charm · 🔮 Charm beats ✨ Spell

Full beats Regular of the same type. Higher value wins ties of the same type.

Winner's Action:

  • ✨ Spell — Place one of your gems on any empty turret.
  • ⚗️ Potion — Swap your gem with any opponent gem.
  • 🔮 Charm — Knock one of your opponent's gems from the tower.
